 Revenue Insights in Hospitality Domain
 Problem Statement
Atliq Grands observed a decline in market share and revenue over a few months.
The Revenue team needed a data-driven solution to identify the root cause and regain business performance.

 Project Objective

To analyze 3 months of hospitality data and build an interactive Power BI dashboard that provides actionable revenue insights.

Suggested improvement areas to potentially regain 20% revenue in the next month

Business Impact

The dashboard enabled the Revenue team to:

Understand revenue drop patterns

Make data-driven decisions

Improve revenue and market share performance
